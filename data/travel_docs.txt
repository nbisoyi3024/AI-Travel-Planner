Travel Guide Knowledge Base
🌍 Paris, France

Overview:
Paris is known for its art, fashion, and culture. It is one of the most visited cities in the world.

Top Attractions:

Eiffel Tower
Louvre Museum
Notre-Dame Cathedral
Montmartre

Best Time to Visit:
April to June and September to October (pleasant weather)

Budget Tips:

Use public transport (Metro)
Visit museums on free-entry days
Stay in budget hotels or hostels

🏝️ Bali, Indonesia

Overview:
Bali is famous for beaches, temples, and vibrant culture.

Top Attractions:

Ubud (rice terraces, temples)
Seminyak (beaches, nightlife)
Uluwatu Temple
Mount Batur

3-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Ubud tour, Monkey Forest
Day 2: Beach day in Seminyak
Day 3: Uluwatu Temple + sunset

Best Time to Visit:
April to October (dry season)

🏙️ New York, USA

Overview:
New York City is a global hub for finance, entertainment, and culture.

Top Attractions:

Statue of Liberty
Central Park
Times Square
Empire State Building

Travel Tips:

Use subway for cheaper travel
Book tickets in advance
Avoid peak tourist seasons for better prices

🇨🇦 Canada Travel Info

Visa Information:

Many travelers require a visa or eTA
Check official government website before traveling

Best Time to Visit:

Summer (June–August) for sightseeing
Winter (December–February) for snow activities

Budget Travel Tips

Book flights in advance
Travel during off-season
Use local transport instead of taxis
Choose budget accommodations (hostels, Airbnb)

General Travel Tips

Always carry valid ID and documents
Keep digital and physical copies of passports
Buy travel insurance
Learn basic local phrases
Respect local culture and laws

Sample 5-Day Europe Itinerary

Day 1: Paris – Eiffel Tower, Seine River
Day 2: Paris – Louvre, Montmartre
Day 3: Travel to Amsterdam – Canal tour
Day 4: Amsterdam – Museums, city exploration
Day 5: Return / Shopping

🌦️ Weather-Based Travel Advice
Summer: Best for beaches and sightseeing
Winter: Ideal for snow sports and northern lights
Rainy Season: Avoid tropical destinations
