World Cities Travel Guide:

- Paris, France: Known for the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and charming streets.
- New York City, USA: Famous for Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty.
- London, UK: Home to Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum.
- Tokyo, Japan: A vibrant mix of modern skyscrapers, temples, and sushi culture.
- Rome, Italy: Historic city with the Colosseum, Vatican City, and ancient ruins.
- Dubai, UAE: Known for Burj Khalifa, luxury shopping, and desert experiences.
- Singapore: Clean and modern city with Marina Bay Sands and Gardens by the Bay.
- Barcelona, Spain: Famous for Gaudi architecture, beaches, and La Sagrada Familia.
- Sydney, Australia: Known for the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.
- Istanbul, Turkey: A cultural blend of East and West with Hagia Sophia and bazaars.
- Bangkok, Thailand: Vibrant city with temples, street food, and nightlife.
- Amsterdam, Netherlands: Known for canals, cycling culture, and museums.
- Cape Town, South Africa: Famous for Table Mountain and scenic coastal views.
- Rio de Janeiro, Brazil: Known for Christ the Redeemer and Copacabana beach.
- Vancouver, Canada: Beautiful city with mountains, parks, and waterfront views.
